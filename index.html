About Me

My name is Austin Stamps. I am 29 years old and live outside of Asheville North Carolina in a small argricultural town called Barnardsville. I have worked previously in several industries including Challenge Course Operation and Construction, Food Service both as back of house and front of house, Retail Sales and working under a General Contractor framing houses and small barns.

I enjoyed all of these positions and had many opportunities to speak with and meet the web developers and emplyees of agencies that advertised for the respective company I was employed by. I realized that these people were the backbone of these buisnesses, without a funtionaing and well designed website and without online advertising the buisness would not have near the profit or reputation it current had. It was at this point that I fell in love with this line of work and decided to pursue it in my personal career.
